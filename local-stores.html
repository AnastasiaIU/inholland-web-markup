Where to buy running gear in the Hague

De Hardloopwinkel

De Hardloopwinkel is more than just a store or a webshop; it's a community of passionate runners with over 25 years of combined experience in the running industry. Founded by Henri Jansen, a former employee at ASICS, and his wife Eva, the shop is driven by a team that truly cares about the runner's experience. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned marathoner, their experts, including Frank who manages their webshop, are ready to offer personalized advice. Henri, Eva, and Frank are not just business owners; they're runners themselves who understand the joys and challenges of the sport. From financial administration to SEO challenges, the team does it all, even as they juggle their own busy lives filled with family, hobbies, and of course, running. With such genuine dedication to helping runners, De Hardloopwinkel is your go-to spot for not just gear, but also community and expert advice.

Decathlon

Decathlon is a one-stop-shop for all your athletic needs, offering an extensive range of high-quality, affordable sports gear and equipment, including running essentials. Known for their in-house brands, Decathlon provides options for runners of all levels, from casual joggers to competitive athletes. The store prioritizes customer experience, featuring knowledgeable staff who are often sports enthusiasts themselves. Whether you're in need of new running shoes, apparel, or accessories like hydration packs, you'll find it all at Decathlon, making it a convenient destination for runners looking for both variety and value.

Bever

Bever is the go-to destination for outdoor enthusiasts, including runners who take their sport beyond the pavement. With a wide range of high-quality gear and apparel tailored for different types of outdoor activities, Bever offers specialized running equipment that's built to withstand the elements. Whether you're looking for trail running shoes, moisture-wicking clothing, or advanced GPS watches, Bever provides a curated selection of products designed for durability and performance. Knowledgeable staff are on hand to offer personalized advice, ensuring you find the right gear to enhance your running experience, no matter the terrain or weather conditions.
