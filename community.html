Communities and events

Zuiderpark parkrun

  • Free for everyone.
  • Every Saturday at 9:00am.
  • 5km route.
  • Bring a barcode for results.

Zuiderpark parkrun is a weekly event that encapsulates the spirit of community running. Taking place every Saturday morning at 9:00am in Zuiderpark, this free 5km event welcomes everyone, regardless of their pace. Whether you're walking, jogging, or running, you'll find a supportive community here. And if you're interested, you can also volunteer to help organize the event. Following the run, participants often gather for coffee, making it not just a fitness event but also a social one. Just remember to register before your first run and bring a scannable barcode to get your results.

The Hague Road Runners (HRR)

  • Weekly trainings.
  • Special events and group trips.
  • Any level of experience.

Welcome to a vibrant community of over 700 runners at The Hague Road Runners (HRR), one of the largest running clubs in the area. Whether you're a beginner looking for a 5km start or an advanced runner training for a marathon, HRR has a group for you. The club offers a variety of activities beyond road running, including trail running, Nordic walking, and hiking. Trainings take place multiple times a week, and the club also organizes special events and group trips. Located near Clingendael Park, the clubhouse features amenities like showers, changing rooms, and a cozy bar. CPC Loop Den Haag

  • A big event with history.
  • Hosted every year.
  • Amateurs and professionals.

Mark your calendars for mid-March and get ready for the CPC Loop Den Haag, an annual half marathon event that has been a tradition in The Hague since 1975. Known for its relatively flat course, the event is conducive to setting fast times, and it has even hosted record-breaking runs, such as Samuel Wanjiru's former men's half marathon world record set in 2007. The event is rich in history and has shifted from European dominance in its early years to featuring standout performances from African athletes in recent decades. Whether you're aiming to set a new personal record or simply enjoy the thrill of the race, the CPC Loop offers a competitive yet welcoming atmosphere for all.
