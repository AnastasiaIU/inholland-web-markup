Where to run in the Hague

Zuiderpark

Zuiderpark is a runner's paradise located in the heart of The Hague. With its vast open spaces and meandering paths, it offers a variety of terrains for both novice and seasoned runners. The park also features a number of recreational facilities, making it an ideal location for those who enjoy combining their run with other fitness activities. Its lush greenery and tranquil atmosphere provide a perfect backdrop for a satisfying run, no matter the time of year.

Haagse Bos

Nestled near the city center, Haagse Bos is a woodland area that offers a natural retreat for runners. Its shaded paths are especially appealing during hot summer days. The trails are mostly flat, making it suitable for runners of all levels. Whether you're looking to focus on your speed, enjoy a leisurely jog, or immerse yourself in nature, Haagse Bos has something for every runner.

Zandmotorpad

While The Hague is well-known for its scenic coastline, Zandmotorpad offers something truly unique for runners. What sets this location apart is its man-made origin and its surrounding sea buckthorn bushes, which lend a refreshing aroma to your run. This tiled pathway takes you on a journey through a distinct part of The Hague's coastline, offering both the stability of a well-constructed path and the sensory experience of the sea and gorgeous flora. It's an ideal spot for those looking to add a new dimension to their running routine.
